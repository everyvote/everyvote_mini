Contact EveryVote

Thank you for your feedback. The goal of EveryVote is to provide free and equal access to online election information resources.

EveryVote helps USA universities share election and candidate information in one convenient online location.

If you would like help setting up an election page for your student organization, please provide your:

  • name
  • email
  • university
  • student organization name
  • title of office (if you hold one)
  • upcoming voting days (if you know them)
  • offices in next election

You can also reach EveryVote on Twitter @EveryVoteU and Facebook www.facebook.com/everyvote.
